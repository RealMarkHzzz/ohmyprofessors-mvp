## üéØ Strategic Highlights

### Core Value Proposition
**"The first Australian professor rating platform built for the post-reform defamation era"**

**Target:** Australian university students making high-stakes unit selections  
**Differentiation:** Legal compliance + verified reviews + AI moderation  
**Moat:** 12-18 month legal infrastructure lead + network effects

---

### Product Positioning

**For:** Students who need professor-specific insights (not just subject reviews)  
**Unlike:** RateMyProfessors (US-focused) and StudentVIP (subject-focused)  
**We Provide:** Legally-safe, verified peer reviews of teaching quality

**User Personas:**
1. **The Informed Optimizer (60%)** - GPA-conscious undergrads researching electives
2. **The International Investor (25%)** - High-fee-paying students seeking ROI
3. **The Grad School Hunter (15%)** - Research students seeking good supervisors

---

### Business Model Evolution

#### Phase 1: Free Growth (Months 1-12)
- **Strategy:** Network effects land grab
- **Revenue:** $0
- **Goal:** 10,000 reviews across Group of Eight universities

#### Phase 2: Freemium (Months 13-18)
- **Premium Tier:** $4.99/month (unlimited access, advanced filters)
- **Conversion Target:** 2% of MAU
- **Revenue:** $50K-$100K ARR

#### Phase 3: Diversified (Months 19-36)
- **Revenue Mix:**
  - 60% Premium subscriptions
  - 25% Ethical advertising (student services)
  - 10% B2B university partnerships
  - 5% Affiliate deals
- **Revenue:** $300K-$500K ARR
- **Break-even:** Month 20

### Competitive Moats

1. **Legal Moat (12-18 month lead)**
   - Proprietary AI moderation trained on Australian defamation law
   - First-mover advantage in post-reform era

2. **Network Effects (Compounding)**
   - 10,000 reviews = defensible threshold
   - More reviews ‚Üí more value ‚Üí more users ‚Üí more reviews

3. **University Partnerships (Lock-In)**
   - B2B contracts create switching costs
   - Data integration into university systems

4. **Brand Trust (Reputation)**
   - "OhMyProfessors = professor ratings" (like Google = search)
   - 24 months to establish brand dominance

5. **Data Advantage (Proprietary)**
   - 3 years of historical trends impossible to replicate
   - Predictive analytics from cumulative data

### Risk Mitigation

#### Top 5 Risks & Contingencies

**Risk 1: Defamation Suit (30% probability)**
- Mitigation: $50K legal fund, $2M insurance, 72-hour takedown SLA
- Contingency: Safe harbor defense, settle quickly if losing

**Risk 2: University Opposition (40% probability)**
- Mitigation: Frame as quality assurance, offer free B2B dashboard
- Contingency: Alternative verification (student ID upload)

**Risk 3: Competitor Clone (60% probability)**
- Mitigation: Speed to 10,000 reviews, lock in partnerships
- Contingency: Double down on Australian-specific features

**Risk 4: Low Contribution Rate (30% probability)**
- Mitigation: Gamification, incentives, timely prompts
- Contingency: Paid review bounties ($2/review)

**Risk 5: Moderation Failure (20% probability)**
- Mitigation: Multi-layer moderation, weekly audits
- Contingency: Halt reviews until system fixed, ratings-only pivot
